Dr. Charles Gueunet

Participant

Kitware

Charles Gueunet joined Kitware in February 2016. For the first three years, he worked on his PhD on the topic of “High Performance Level-set based Topological Data Analysis”, and became one of the main contributors of the Topology ToolKit (TTK). After defending in February 2019, Charles joined the Scientific Visualization team at Kitware. He now works on various projects involving parallel programming, discrete geometry and data analysis algorithms.
